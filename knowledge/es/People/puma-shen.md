# Shen Pei-yang: He Studied China's Cognitive Warfare, Then China Put Him on Satellite Imagery

In the afternoon of a day in the 2010s, a law doctoral student in his thirties followed a prison warden into the Taipei Youth Observation Center. The floor had just been mopped, and the water marks were not yet dry. The warden stepped over them, and the delegation followed. He stopped and took a detour. Two youths who were mopping looked up and bowed to him, saying softly, "Thank you."[^1]

When he returned home that day, he wrote a question in his notebook: "Why do our attitudes toward people vary so greatly in different places?"[^1] This question later led him from criminal law to criminology, from academia to civilian defense, from researching China's cognitive warfare to being investigated by Chinese public security organs, warned by CCTV that "the next one is you," and having his residence coordinates in Taipei published by a Chinese Weibo account using commercial satellite imagery.

His name is Shen Pei-yang. Stage name Puma. Born in 1982 in Taipei, he is an Associate Professor and Director of the Institute of Criminology at National Taipei University. He studies the CCP's information warfare against Taiwan, cognitive warfare, and transnational repression. He studied these things for seven years; in the eighth year, these things came back for him.

> **30-Second Overview:** Shen Pei-yang (born 1982) is an Associate Professor and Director of the Institute of Criminology at National Taipei University. In 2021, he co-founded the [Black Bear Academy](https://kuma-academy.org/) with strategic scholar Ho Cheng-hui; Tsai Hsing-cheng donated 600 million NTD to support it, with the goal of training 3 million "Black Bear Warriors."[^3] In February 2024, he was elected as a DPP proportional representation legislator.[^4] From October 2024, he was sanctioned by China six times within a year; on October 28, 2025, he became the first elected Taiwanese political figure investigated by China under the "crime of splitting the country,"[^5] and on November 9, 2025, CCTV aired a 7.5-minute "dossier" special warning "the next one is you."[^6] On New Year's Day 2026, a Chinese Weibo account used commercial satellite imagery to publish the coordinates of his residence and workplace in Taipei.[^7] He continued to fly to The Hague, Germany, and France; on Valentine's Day 2026, he spent it on a flight to France.[^8]

## "Do Not Interrupt or Refute When the Teacher Is Speaking"

On Shen Pei-yang's elementary school contact book, a teacher wrote: "**Do not interrupt or refute when the teacher is speaking. Pei-yang is not bad by nature, but occasionally loses control and is arrogant.**" His mother replied behind it: "**He indeed has a bad attitude and is arrogant.** In middle school, he gave the middle finger to a teacher."[^1] When he was 37 years old and interviewed by Mirror Media, he still had an afro, T-shirt, and shorts. His favorite character was Yang Guo—"**Complete rebellion, I like this look the most.**"[^1]

He went from Fuxing Kindergarten, Fuxing Elementary School, and Fuxing Middle School all the way up, graduating from Fuxing High School and entering the National Taiwan University Department of Law.[^9] In middle school, he already perceived the existence of class: "Fuxing students discussed newly opened Japanese restaurants, Fuxing High School students ate pork rib bento boxes, they ate brown sugar ice, which is white ice with only sugar water added."[^1] In college, he started his tutoring life, with the stage name "Puma" because it was easier to remember.[^10] This stage name followed him for twenty years—from the tutoring industry, academia, think tanks, to the Legislative Yuan. All his official social media accounts now use [@pumashen](https://www.threads.com/@pumashen).[^11]

What led him to shift from "a lawyer who teaches criminal law" to criminology were two scenes.

The first scene was his senior's supermarket case. His senior's client was a large supermarket operator, suing a fruit farmer. "The farmer was scared, agreed to settle, but the amount was high; basically, one family was ruined." His senior said to Shen Pei-yang: "The law is just written this way, who told him to break the law."[^1] Shen Pei-yang wrote: "**The longer you study law, the more likely you are to become someone you originally didn't want to be.**"[^1]

The second scene was the youth observation center mopping incident. The youths bowed to him, and he couldn't help but think: the same youths, outside they are the ones being chased, here they are the ones being sympathized with; what scale you use to look at a person determines what you do to them.

> **📝 Curator's Note**
> A scholar who later studied state violence, his academic intuition about "how people are treated" started with the bowing of two mopping youths. More than ten years later, he was investigated by the CCP, made into a fake documentary by CCTV, and he never wrote himself as a victim. This sense of proportion likely started from that day at the youth observation center.

## Puma and Two Choices to Leave the US

Shen Pei-yang's educational timeline is as simple as a boarding pass stub. NTU Law LL.B. (2004), University of Pennsylvania Law LL.M. (2007), NTU Criminal Law LL.M. (2008), UC Irvine Criminology, Law, and Society PhD (2017). His doctoral thesis advisors were two heavyweight scholars in white-collar crime research, Henry Pontell and Elliott Currie.[^12]

During those five years in Irvine, he lived in a city with "the best public security in the US"—"streets all look the same, confusing thieves, doors don't need locks."[^1] When he graduated in 2017, he received tenured faculty offers from US schools like North Carolina State University.[^13] His five thesis committee professors advised him to return to Taiwan: "You care so much about Taiwan, why not go back."[^1]

His explanation for choosing to return to Taiwan was short: "**The air is bad anyway, I've been polluted since childhood, I've eaten plasticizers. It's just my own home.**"[^1]

He returned to Taipei in 2017, becoming an assistant professor at the Institute of Criminology, National Taipei University. In August 2023, he succeeded as the institute director.[^2] The university page lists his research areas as criminal law, sociology of law, criminal policy, and white-collar crime.[^2] His doctoral training was actually not purely in a law school—UC Irvine placed his training in the **School of Social Ecology**, spanning criminology, urban planning, public policy, and environmental psychology.[^14] This training later determined his perspective on studying the CCP's cognitive warfare—cutting not from legal articles, but from "how people are influenced in the environment."

Two years later, he began to point this perspective in another direction.

## "Beating Taiwan is Worse Than Deceiving Taiwan"

In 2018, Shen Pei-yang began systematically collecting evidence of the CCP's information warfare against Taiwan. He split the CCP's united front work against Taiwan into five lines: Ministry of State Security, United Front Work Department, Taiwan Affairs Office, PLA, and Communist Youth League.[^15] Each line has its own budget, tools, and rhythm. This analytical framework later became the standard language for understanding cognitive warfare in Taiwan.

In 2019, he co-founded the [Taiwan Democracy Lab](https://doublethinklab.org/) (Doublethink Lab) with民间 researchers, serving as chairman.[^16] On April 25, 2022, they released the [China Index](https://china-index.io/)—a "China Influence Index" covering over 80 countries, nine dimensions (media, arts, economy, society and culture, military, law enforcement, technology, politics, diplomatic affairs) with a total of 99 indicators.[^17] This index remains one of the most frequently cited databases of the CCP's external influence in international political and media circles. Shen Pei-yang himself later used this data as a basis to testify before the [US-China Economic and Security Review Commission (USCC)](https://www.uscc.gov/sites/default/files/2023-03/Puma_Shen_Testimony.pdf) commissioned by the US Congress in 2023.[^18]

In July 2024, Shen Pei-yang and Doublethink Lab researcher Wu Ming-hsuan co-published _Beating Taiwan is Worse Than Deceiving Taiwan: Q&A on China's Cognitive Warfare Against Taiwan_ (Locus Publishing). The title itself is the argument. For the CCP, using military force is too expensive; deceiving Taiwanese people into not voting or voting wrong is too cheap.[^19] This book was repeatedly cited in the political sections of CNA, United Daily News, and Liberty Times. "Beating Taiwan is worse than deceiving Taiwan" became the abbreviation for describing the CCP's strategy against Taiwan.

But writing a book wouldn't lead to a CCP public security investigation. What made him an "enemy" was another matter.

## 3 Million Black Bear Warriors

At the end of 2021, Shen Pei-yang co-founded the [Black Bear Academy](https://kuma-academy.org/) (Kuma Academy) with Ho Cheng-hui, a researcher at the Taiwan Strategic Simulation Society.[^20] The academy's goal is "national defense education for all"—teaching civilian defense, cybersecurity, fraud prevention, AED/CPR,止血 bandaging, and evacuation response, things usually only for military or professional rescuers, to every Taiwanese willing to learn.[^21]

On September 1, 2022, Tsai Hsing-cheng, former chairman of United Microelectronics Corporation (UMC), who had left the Chinese market due to his advocacy for Taiwan's sovereignty, held a press conference announcing a donation of 600 million NTD to the Black Bear Academy, part of his larger anti-CCP pro-Taiwan donation plan.[^22] Shen Pei-yang said at the press conference: "I hope every household has a 'Black Bear Warrior' with relevant knowledge to protect family safety in critical moments."[^23] The Black Bear Academy's goal was set: **Train 3 million "Black Bear Warriors" within three years**.[^24]

What does 3 million mean? Taiwan's population is about 23 million; excluding children, elderly, and those with mobility issues, 3 million is about one-fifth of the adult population. Tsai Hsing-cheng's 600 million NTD was executed in phased disbursements—this money was designed to be audited, not just a pretty press conference number.[^25]

In September 2022, the Black Bear Academy's first basic camp opened for registration and quickly filled up.[^26] Courses included: firefighting, search and rescue, first aid, evacuation, ICT security, information identification, and public order maintenance, plus AED/CPR, injury assessment, and止血 bandaging.[^27] Outdoor training "Blue Magpie Action" is held regularly, simulating the evacuation and first aid of non-combatants during wartime.[^28]

It is worth mentioning that Shen Pei-yang himself is not the legal representative of the Black Bear Academy. He wrote in a 2024 X post: "I wish I could be (Black Bear Academy) responsible, but the problem is regulations and DPP internal rules, I and my family cannot be responsible."[^29] His role as deputy executive director of the DPP think tank and later as a legislator required him to maintain a legal distance from the NGO he co-founded.

> **📝 Curator's Note**
> A national defense system designed with a criminological framework: one in seven adults knows how to save themselves, spot fake news, and止血 when water and electricity are cut. Shen Pei-yang's research describes how the enemy comes; the Black Bear Academy's courses train how to hold on after being attacked. Two things driven by the same person, this symmetry is a bit coldly abnormal.

## The Sixth Time in One Year

On November 15, 2023, the DPP announced the 2024 proportional representation legislator list, with Shen Pei-yang ranked second, only after children's rights advocate Lin Yueh-chin.[^30] He was elected on January 13, 2024, and took office on February 1, 2024, as the 11th Legislative Yuan legislator.[^4] He led the DPP caucus in promoting ten national security laws (Cross-Strait Act, Hong Kong/Macau Act, Nationality Law, National Security Law, Communication Security Act, Anti-Infiltration Act, Cybersecurity Act, Criminal Law, Military Criminal Law, National Intelligence Work Law).[^31] Starting in 2025, he served as the Convener of the Legislative Yuan Foreign and Defense Committee.[^32]

But on October 14 that year, Taiwan Affairs Office spokesperson Chen Bin-hua announced: Shen Pei-yang, Tsai Hsing-cheng, and the Black Bear Academy itself were listed on the "Stubborn Separatists" list, banning the individuals and their families from entering mainland China, Hong Kong, and Macau.[^33] Shen Pei-yang was named for the first time.

From that day on, the escalation ladder went up step by step. Shen Pei-yang himself summarized the whole process in one sentence in a Facebook post on October 28, 2025: "**This is the 6th time in one year.**"[^34]

| Date       | Event                                                                                                                                         |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2024-10-14 | Taiwan Affairs Office lists Shen Pei-yang, Tsai Hsing-cheng, Black Bear Academy on "Stubborn Separatists" list[^33]                           |
| 2025-06-05 | Father Shen Tu-cheng's Zhao Yi Co. is sanctioned by the CCP, banned from trading with mainland enterprises/individuals[^35]                   |
| 2025-07-16 | Chinese Customs cancels Shen family enterprise Sicuens International Co. Ltd registration[^36]                                                |
| 2025-10-28 | Chongqing Public Security Bureau investigates Shen Pei-yang for "suspected crime of splitting the country," providing a reporting hotline[^5] |
| 2025-11-09 | CCTV News releases 7.5-minute "dossier" Shen Pei-yang special video, warning "stop it, otherwise the next one is you"[^6][^37]                |

These six steps took only thirteen months. The legal basis for the Chongqing Public Security Bureau's notice is the Criminal Law plus the CCP's June 2024 _Opinions on Punishing Stubborn Separatists for Splitting the Country and Inciting Splitting the Country Crimes_ (commonly known as the "22 Articles on Punishing Separatism").[^38] The police notice also listed a reporting email `CQGACTD@163.com` and phone `023-65697660`, inviting anyone to provide "clues."[^39]

On the day of the investigation, Shen Pei-yang responded on Facebook:

> "This is the 6th time in one year, this time directly investigated by public security, the next step is probably wanted, trial in absentia; it doesn't matter, anyway 'Taiwanese are not afraid.'"[^34]
>
> "**Solving the person who raises the problem, solving the person who defends Taiwan, indeed very Communist Party.**"[^34]

In June 2025, when his father's company was sanctioned, he wrote with the same tone: "Very honored, sanctioned three times in one year."[^40]

## CCTV's "Global Capture" Threat

Twelve days after the investigation, on the evening of November 9, 2025, CCTV News Channel aired a 7.5-minute (many Taiwan media wrote 8 minutes) "Dossier Shen Pei-yang" special. CCTV claimed Shen Pei-yang spread statements that "China will militarily unify Taiwan" through the Black Bear Academy and trained "violent Taiwan independence分子," ending with a warning tone: "**Stop it, otherwise the next one is you.**"[^41][^37]

The Mainland Affairs Council (MAC) defined this content as "transnational repression" at its press conference that day—the purpose is not to catch people, but to force Taiwanese society to self-censor before speaking.[^42] Shen Pei-yang also posted on Facebook that night:

> "Don't do what I predict you will do every time, okay? **Be creative.**"[^43]
>
> "China acted faster, wanting to stop me from leaving the country by way of a warrant. **CCTV did a large portion, a 7.5-minute fake documentary about me, threatening global wanted status.**"[^37]
>
> "Whether I am wanted or not, **Nobody cares, Taiwanese really don't care about the CCP.**"[^37]

He also posted a short video, the lens facing CCTV's screenshot: "Taiwan and China are **one country on each side**. Don't妄想 using fabricated charges to extend your hands to Taiwan."[^44]

International reactions gathered within two days. Human Rights Watch Asia Deputy Director Maya Wang issued a statement on October 31, calling the Chongqing Public Security case a Chinese law of transnational abuse of power.[^45] Multiple international human rights organizations supported him simultaneously. The US State Department publicly expressed concern.[^47] [IPAC](https://ipac.global/) (Inter-Parliamentary Alliance on China) co-founder Luke de Pulford posted public support.[^48]

The Legislative Yuan itself moved. On November 19, 2025, the Legislative Yuan Foreign and Defense Committee (the very committee Shen Pei-yang served as convener of) passed a condemnation motion proposed by Wang Ting-yu and others. Wang Ting-yu said in his speech: "**Even if opinions differ, one still has the right to be protected by the state and the constitution.** The People's Republic of China has no right to conduct such political actions against anyone for such baseless reasons."[^49]

China had not yet listed Shen Pei-yang on the official Hong Kong version of the _National Security Law_ bounty list; the highest bounty at the same time was for [YouTuber "Ba Jiong"](/people/八炯) and "Minnan Wolf" at 250,000 RMB.[^50] But the combination of "crime of splitting the country" investigation + CCTV "global wanted" threat had already pushed Shen Pei-yang close to that position.

## The Screw at the Doorstep

As the CCP escalated, Shen Pei-yang's private life was also being escalated.

At the end of August 2025, he publicly revealed for the first time on host Cheng Hung-yi's _Talk Era Figures_ program that he was being followed "all the time." The most direct incident happened at his doorstep: a motorcycle's "screw" was actually a pinhole camera, with the battery hidden under the seat, disguised as a screw. Shen Pei-yang took the license plate, called the police, but unexpectedly, within less than an hour, that motorcycle disappeared; the license plate tracking is still a dead end.[^51]

Even harder to digest were the threatening letters. The letter included a photo of Shen Pei-yang and his wife Tseng Hsin-hui, **with a "death" character written on Tseng Hsin-hui's clothes**, threatening to slit their daughter's throat.[^51] Shen Pei-yang said on the program: "This is very vicious, but this is what the Communist Party would do, we were already prepared in our hearts." His wife Tseng Hsin-hui's reaction was anxiety, insomnia, health deterioration, and social phobia.[^51]

> **📝 Curator's Note**
> Shen Pei-yang's daughter is not his biological child. He and his wife Tseng Hsin-hui adopted through the Luminous Foundation, a two-year process, Shen Pei-yang welcomed a 13-month-old baby girl in 2021; the couple explained the biological mother's role to the child using the concept of "belly mom."[^52]
> The child he chose to accept later became the crossed-out coordinate on the threatening letter sent to his home by the CCP.

## The Hague, Satellite, Valentine's Day

But he did not hide. After November, he instead began to travel abroad intensively.

On November 12, 2025, he attended the [German Bundestag](https://www.bundestag.de/) hearing "The Threat of Fake Information from Dictatorships to Democracy and Human Rights," testifying as an expert witness and legislator. After the hearing, he was interviewed: "Taiwan should not fear the Chinese threat, **everyone must be fearless, and stand with the camp of world democracy and freedom.**"[^53]

Nine days later, on November 21, he arrived in The Hague, Netherlands, attending the 209th Executive Committee of the [Liberal International (LI)](https://liberal-international.org/) and the The Hague session of the [Inter-Parliamentary Alliance on China (IPAC)](https://ipac.global/) with DPP legislator [Fan Yun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fan_Yun). He took a photo at the door of the International Criminal Court (ICC).[^54] He captioned it with four words: "Not afraid." The next day he spoke on LI's "Defending Democracy in an Era of Political Manipulation" panel, attaching an academic label to China's threat:

> "China really wants to stop me from continuing these international participations, so it keeps trying to make me unable to leave the country through sanctions. **These actions prove exactly that China is a paper tiger**—mostly symbolic propaganda for domestic audiences, Taiwan should not be afraid."[^55]
>
> (Pointing to the ICC building behind him) "**This is where dictators are tried for war crimes or genocide.**"[^55]

On New Year's Day 2026, the CCP escalated.

A Chinese Weibo account with many followers, "Gu Yan Mu Chan," posted a threat to hunt down Shen Pei-yang,[^59] claiming to have self-funded custom high-resolution imagery from the commercial satellite "Jilin-1," publishing satellite images of Shen Pei-yang's residence in Taipei (near Da'an Forest Park) and workplace (Section 1, Ji Nan Road, near the Legislative Yuan).[^7] The Chinese state media "Today's Strait" followed with a repost.[^7] Two days later, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a condemnation statement, with wording different from before:

> "After the Chinese state media threatened to globally capture our legislator Shen Pei-yang, recently it further used **the despicable method of digital authoritarianism like doxxing**, threatening the safety of our nationals... not only violating the _Universal Declaration of Human Rights_ and the _International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights_ regarding 'anyone's privacy, family, home shall not be subjected to arbitrary interference,' but also comprehensively persecuting personal privacy, **losing the bottom line of civilization, deeply contemptible.**"[^56]

The National Police Agency announced the same day that it would fully protect Shen Pei-yang's safety.[^57] The Digital Ministry requested Meta and Google to handle it; related posts, videos, and images were taken down within a few days.[^58]

Shen Pei-yang himself responded on Facebook on January 3:

> "**What China marked is my coordinates, but what it shows is their collective inferiority complex towards democratic Taiwan.**"[^59]
>
> "For those who were mocking dashcams a few days ago, I won't call you accomplices, because your level is still far off. But academically, there is a specific term for this: '**Useful Idiots**'."[^59]

Two weeks later, on January 24, he posted a long article on Facebook, splitting the CCP's 2026 "lock Taiwan" into three axes: physically disarming (Lan-Bai parties cutting diplomatic and defense budgets), spiritually changing cognition (doubt-US theory affecting about 5% of key voters), and election resource monopoly (party assets and media-related laws). At the end of the article, he wrote: "**My biggest function should still be 'target'**."[^60]

The word "target" appeared repeatedly in his narrative afterwards, a label he attached to himself.

On February 7, he flew to France for an 8-day diplomatic mission. The key point of the French background: [Interpol](https://www.interpol.int/) headquarters location, and France has an extradition agreement with China—he theoretically was at the highest risk in such countries, yet he still proceeded as planned. The mission content was exchanging Taiwan's Ministry of National Defense's new _National Defense Handbook_, cognitive warfare response, social media and cyber warfare, and national resilience. France itself issued a similar national risk response handbook in 2025; Shen Pei-yang described France's anti-fake news units as having borrowed design from Taiwan.[^8]

Valentine's Day (February 14) he spent on the plane. After returning, he wrote on Facebook: "**Despite the pressure of being globally wanted by Beijing, I still completed the 8-day diplomatic mission as planned. However, I ultimately spent Valentine's Day on the plane.** There is another one next! I will stand at the forefront with my country."[^61]

## From Target to Candidate

In early April 2026, the DPP Election Strategy Committee held a meeting. [United Daily News](https://udn.com/) political section reported that insiders revealed the party planned to push Shen Pei-yang to run for Taipei Mayor.[^62] By mid-to-late April, it was almost a certainty. Former Minister of the Interior Hsu Kuo-yung publicly endorsed him.[^63] TVBS December 2025 polls showed incumbent Taipei Mayor Chiang Wan-an's support at about 64%, Shen Pei-yang at about 22%.[^64] A significant gap, but election outcomes are not within the scope of this article.

On April 25, he cut off the afro he had for over ten years, appearing on stage with Chiang Wan-an for the first time at the Songshan Ci Hui Temple Mother-Child Culture Festival.[^65] Yang Guo cut his hair short.

From the bowing of the mopping youths to CCTV's fake documentary, from research on a desk to coordinates on satellite imagery, from "cannot be Black Bear Academy responsible" to "maybe run for Taipei Mayor," Shen Pei-yang's position changed many times. He used the same sentence to footnote each change: "I am a target." This sentence is both self-mockery and a calm academic observation. In the CCP's narrative against Taiwan, he indeed is the one to be targeted. A scholar who takes the label of the object he studies and sticks it on himself, continuing to work; few people can do this.

## Where Are the Bowing Youths Now

Back to that mopping afternoon. The moment Shen Pei-yang stopped, he detoured around the water marks. The youths bowed to him. More than ten years later, he sat on the other end of the Chongqing Public Security Bureau police notice, his wife's clothes crossed with a "death" character, his home's satellite coordinates publicly named by a Chinese Weibo account.

He himself wrote a short sentence in June 2025. On the day his father's company was sanctioned, he responded on Facebook with four words: "**Very honored.**"[^40]

The question from that day at the youth observation center is still on him: "Why do our attitudes toward people vary so greatly in different places?" He studied this question for twenty years. In the end, the things he studied came for him, marking him as prey. But he studied long enough, knowing **prey does not need to become a victim first**, can become the questioner first—so from The Hague to the German Parliament, to France, each stop used the same grammar to say the same thing: you can do this to me, and I continue to do my work.

In late April 2026, he cut his hair, preparing to fight another election. But on the CCP's satellite imagery, the target coordinates remain in place.
