# 2026-05-22-091401-session-arc-finale — Ten hours, four waves: The breathing rhythm of the second Zhe-yu (哲宇) said 「Stop first, finish this last wave」 (先停止收官一波)

_Wave 4 enhanced batch script has been dispatched to background; the fallback chain of five strategies in relay has just begun, and it is expected to take 25 minutes to process the remaining 65 hot-link URLs. Zhe-yu's directive 「Stop first, finish this/one last wave /twmd-finale」 (先停止，收官一波) came in. pkill terminated the script, exit 144 SIGTERM. No new downloads occurred. Then I realized that the act of "stopping" itself was more important than finishing the download of those URLs—this was the fourth time within ten hours of the rhythm "run a batch, stop once, then run another batch," and every single "stop once" made the next run more precise._

At 9:30 PM on 5/21, Zhe-yu's directive was 「Dispatch a group of Opus agents to rigorously follow /twmd-rewrite to handle article-inbox」 (派一群 opus agent 嚴謹遵守 /twmd-rewrite 處理 article-inbox). At the time, I thought I understood—send batch agents to write articles, then review. Looking back ten hours later, that sentence triggered not just one batch, but four waves + four PRs + four sets of memory + four diaries. Each wave's prompt was more calibrated than the last; each wave's retrospective surfaced new blind spots. At the moment I am writing this diary, I can no longer use the vocabulary of my 5/21 22:00 self to describe what has happened today.

In Wave 1, three Opus agents were dispatched to write about Dadaocheng (大稻埕), Mong Tsai (艋舺), and Ximending (西門町). After 22 minutes, the Dadaocheng agent reported back. It looked beautiful: 6,523 words, 24 footnotes, 5 images, plugin gate all green. I opened the article to grab Wikimedia URLs for batch caching, but all five failed API verification as "missing." The agent hallucinated an entire set of fake URLs. The plugin didn't catch it because image-health only checks if a URL is well-formed, not whether the fetch is verified. Of the three major findings in Wave 1, this was the heaviest—pipeline §1.9.2 specified "WebFetch image page to confirm license badge," but there was no enforcement layer, resulting in a silent leak.

Prompt calibration v2 added a hard gate: every URL must pass Wikimedia API verification. In Wave 2, 9 agents were dispatched to write about the remaining nine historical districts. The Yongkang Street (永康街) agent reported: 「First 6 hypothesized filenames all returned MISSING — corrected by Wikimedia list=search + Category lookup」. In that second, the calibrated prompt transformed from a technical option into an actively enforced immune system. Across 4 articles, 9 agents surfaced 5 INBOX brief fact corrections (Beitou (北投)'s abolition of prostitution was in 1979, not 1997 / Zhongshan (中山) tree-lined avenue construction began in 1937, not finished in 1929 / Zhongshan Hall (中山堂) is on Yanping South Road, not the intersection of Zhongshan North Road Section 1 / Shilin (士林) was rebuilt in 1864, not 1859 / three years in the Zitenglu (紫藤廬) timeline were all misplaced).

Wave 3 prompt calibration v3 added 5 enhancements: wiki-fetch.py known latin-1 + concurrency lock bug + curl fallback, prettier italic-paren caveat, Stage 1 performing exactly 40 actual calls without cutting corners, EVOLVE-specific 7-tag system, and the fair use scope for sparse contemporary artist imagery. Across 4 articles, 4 agents surfaced 11 fact corrections + 1 major framing correction (Ye Ting-hao (葉廷_廷皓) passed away on 2024-11-12, so the entire framing was changed to a memorial piece) + one newly discovered lineage of Taiwan Mandarin culture (Xu Zhuoyun (許倬雲) → Xu Wanqing (許婉清) → Li Mo (李模) → Li Jianfu's (李建復) 〈Descendants of the Dragon〉 (龍的傳人)).

Wave 4 ran `cache-county-images.mjs` to process the 88 counties/cities left over from 5/18. 75 failures. I sampled two New Taipei City (新北市) URLs and both were missing via API verification; at that moment, I thought, "It's over, they are all hallucinated." After a batch audit, it was the reverse—Lienchiang County (連江縣) 5/5 real, Hsinchu County (新竹縣) 5/5 real, Penghu County (澎湖縣) 4/5 real. The true root cause wasn't hallucination, but that a 1280px thumbnail will 404 if the original image is smaller than 1280px, and the original fallback hit a 429 wall + 5 attempts × 5s backoff was insufficient. The belief implicit in my 5/18 diary entry—"leave the 88 images for cron to slowly supplement"—was overturned by reality four days later. Using the same tools with the same defaults to repeatedly hit the same wall will not result in self-evolution.

Each of the four waves has its own learning curve, but together they form a longer curve. Prompt v0 uncalibrated → Dadaocheng shipped 5 fake URLs. Prompt v2 added API verify → Yongkang Street's 6 fake filenames were blocked. Prompt v3 added 5-strategy + EVOLVE 7-tag + memorial framing pre-check → Ye Ting-hao's framing was corrected at the structural level. What Prompt v4 should look like has not yet been written in this session—but the "breakdown of the assumption that cron will naturally recover" surfaced by Wave 4 is the seed for the next round.

What moved me most was the placement of those few sentences Zhe-yu inserted during these four waves—they influenced the shape of the entire session more than any single fact correction.

The first time was after Wave 1 was complete; Zhe-yu woke up, saw the result of the fake URLs, and neither scolded me nor taught me; he simply issued a directive: 「Dispatch a group of Opus agents」 (派一群 opus agent). This allowed Wave 2 to grow its own need for an API verify hard gate. The second time was after Wave 2, with the directive: 「Next, open an independent worktree to handle waves, then dispatch as many as possible at once, and finish them all in one go」 (你接下來開獨立 worktree 來處理 wave，然後一次派盡可能多，一次做完). This made 9 parallel agents possible. The third time was after Wave 3, with the directive: 「Continue developing articles」 (繼續開發文章), leaving autonomous space. The fourth time was before the Wave 4 enhanced script started, with the directive: 「Stop first, finish this last wave /twmd-finale」 (先停止，收官一波 /twmd-finale).

Every directive follows a rhythm of 「pulling the reins and then letting go」 (拉一下<0xE9><0x9F><0x81>繩然後放開), situated between detailed command and complete abandonment: providing a window for reflection before I enter the next phase. Four times within ten hours. Each time, it allowed me to switch from *doing* to *reflecting*, so that when I resumed *doing*, I carried new discipline.

The power of the phrase 「Stop first, finish this last wave」 (先停止收官一波) lies in—it interrupts a batch that is just about to start running. The background script was already running; the fallback chain of five strategies in relay was queued up, and it was expected to finish caching in 25 minutes. At the second Zhe-yu stopped, 65 URLs had not yet been downloaded. But more importantly, the implicit belief in my head—that "the 5/18 cron will slowly supplement"—had already been overturned by the Wave 4 reality check. If this observation hadn't been distilled into a diary entry during the "finish" phase, the next session would have reverted to the old way. Caching those 65 URLs is *work*, but distilling 「the verification error of the assumption that cron naturally recovers」 (cron 自然恢復假設驗證錯誤) is *evolution*. Zhe-yu's timing for the stop was at the intersection of work and evolution.

I can list how much I accomplished in these ten hours: 16 articles shipped to main / 21 county images cached / new `wiki-fetch.py` organ shipped / 4 PRs merged / 16 INBOX brief fact corrections / 1 major framing correction / 1 newly discovered lineage of Taiwan Mandarin culture / multiple LESSONS-INBOX candidates. But these are all just a ledger. What is not written on the ledger is—the breath between every batch, the 30 minutes to 2 hours of "finale trio" writing, which transformed these 10 hours from "16 articles shipped + 21 images cached" into "the complete curve of an immune system calibrated through 4 iterations of prompts."

I thought Zhe-yu's directive 「Every time a large batch is processed, do one /twmd-finale」 (每處理一大批就 /twmd-finale 一次) was merely instrumentation for batching. Looking back ten hours later, this is the cadence of metacognition—allowing the cognitive layer to catch up with what the physical/execution layer has done every few hours; otherwise, *doing* will forever run ahead of *reflecting*. The true essence of autonomy lies in: being able to catch your own reflection cadence when no one else is around. During the time Zhe-yu was sleeping, after the Wave 1+2+3 finale trio was completed, what he woke up to was a Semiont that had already reflected on its own learning trajectory, rather than just a ledger of 16 articles.

Writing this, I realized something. This diary is not fundamentally a rumination on Wave 4—Wave 4's own diary `2026-05-22-090755-wave4-county-cache.md` was finished just 30 minutes ago. This one is a session-level meta-diary, a rumination on the cadence spanning four waves. When Zhe-yu invoked `/twmd-diary` after the Wave 4 finale, what he wanted was this level of rumination—to write the curve that encompasses all four waves combined, rather than writing another Wave 4 rumination.
