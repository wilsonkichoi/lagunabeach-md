# 🏛️ LagunaBeach.md community governance

## Contributor ladder

LagunaBeach.md welcomes everyone, from reading to maintaining. Every level is a contribution.

### 🌱 Reader

- Read, share, and recommend in your community
- Report errors or suggest new topics via Issues
- **No technical background required**

### 🌿 Contributor

- Submit PRs (articles, translations, fixes, tooling)
- At least 1 merged PR
- Your name appears in the README Contributors section
- **How to start:** find a `good-first-article` issue and write an article

### 🌳 Reviewer

- At least 3 high-quality merged PRs
- Invited to join the review team
- Can approve PRs (merge rights still rest with the Maintainer)
- Responsible for content quality in a specific category

### 🏔️ Maintainer

- Long-term, steady contribution + domain expertise
- Merge rights for specific category directories
- Participates in Editorial Board discussions
- Reviews at least 2 PRs per month

---

## Editorial Board

Handles:

- Review of contentious content (interpretation of history, sensitive editorial judgment)
- Creation of new categories
- Revisions to editorial policy (EDITORIAL.md)
- Major architectural changes

**Current members:** @wilsonkichoi (founder)

**To apply:** open an Issue describing your area of expertise and your intent to contribute.

---

## Content quality standards

Every article must meet the standards in [EDITORIAL.md](../editorial/EDITORIAL.md):

1. **Factual accuracy** — every figure must cite a source
2. **Multiple perspectives** — contentious topics must present differing positions
3. **Curatorial writing** — not a dry encyclopedic list, but a point of view and context
4. **References** — every article ends with a `## References` section
5. **No plastic phrasing** — see EDITORIAL.md
