## Officially Joining Fubon in 2026

Before the 2026 season, Fubon announced that Park Sung-eun had joined Fubon Angels, forming the Korean Five Pillars alongside Lee Ju-yeon, Lee Ya-ying, Nam Min-jung, and Lee Hao-cheng. This configuration has allowed Fubon's cheering system to mature on both the buzz and operational fronts.

Her official debut came at the Xinzhuang home opener in April, when in an interview she admitted to nerves. That public statement was remembered by many fans, because it gave a human quality to the "goddess image."
