## Selection Principles

1. **International Visibility**: International-level achievement or fame in their field
2. **Historical Position**: Pioneering or milestone contributions to Taiwan in some field
3. **Diverse Representation**: Balanced gender, ethnicity, generation, and field distribution
4. **Include Controversial Figures**: Political figures from across the spectrum, presenting complete range
5. **Indigenous Representatives**: A-mei, Syaman Rapongan and other indigenous figures prioritized

## Article Quality Standards

- 800-1500 words in Chinese
- 3-5 `##` sections
- frontmatter: title/description/date/tags
- **Must include references section + clickable URLs** (minimum 3)
- Prohibit plastic phrases ("in the tide of..." "not only...but also")
- Factual accuracy (years, awards, achievements need cross-verification)
- Use `[[Name]]` wikilinks to connect other existing figures
