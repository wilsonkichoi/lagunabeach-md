# Taiwan Official Website Resources

Taiwan.md official website resources is the most complete index of official/authoritative websites in Taiwan, covering government agencies, culture and education, tourism, technology and economy, media and news, and more.

## Resource Categories

### 🏛️ Official Agencies

- **Central Government**: Office of the President, Executive Yuan, Legislative Yuan, Judicial Yuan, and the other branches of the five-Yuan system
- **Ministries and Agencies**: Ministry of Health and Welfare, Ministry of Labor, Ministry of Environment, Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of National Defense, Ministry of Justice, and more
- **Local Governments**: Six special municipality governments (Taipei, New Taipei, Taichung, Kaohsiung, Tainan, Taoyuan)
- **Special Agencies**: National Development Council (NDC), Mainland Affairs Council (MAC), Council of Indigenous Peoples (CIP), and more

### 🎭 Culture and Education

- **Museums**: National Palace Museum, National Museum of Taiwan History, National Museum of Natural Science, National Museum of Marine Biology & Aquarium, National Museum of Taiwan Literature
- **Top Universities**: National Taiwan University (NTU), National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), National Chengchi University (NCCU), National Taiwan Normal University (NTNU)
- **Research Institutions**: Academia Sinica, National Science and Technology Council (NSTC)
- **Arts and Culture Institutions**: National Theater & Concert Hall, National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts (Weiwuying), Taiwan Creative Content Agency (TAICCA)

### ✈️ Tourism and Travel

- **National Parks**: Yushan, Taroko, Kenting, Shei-Pa, Yangmingshan
- **Transportation**: Taiwan Railways Administration (TRA), Taiwan High Speed Rail (THSR), Taiwan Shuttle Bus
- **Official Agencies**: Tourism Administration, National Immigration Agency, Ministry of Transportation and Communications, Weather Administration

### 💼 Technology and Economy

- **Leading Companies**: TSMC, UMC, Foxconn (Hon Hai), Delta Electronics
- **R&D Institutions**: Institute for Information Industry (III), National Applied Research Laboratories (NARLabs), Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI)
- **Financial Institutions**: Taiwan Stock Exchange (TWSE), Taipei Exchange (TPEx), Financial Supervisory Commission (FSC)
- **Government Agencies**: Ministry of Economic Affairs, International Trade Administration, Small and Medium Enterprise and Startup Administration

### 📰 Media and News

- **Official Media**: Central News Agency (CNA), Public Television Service (PTS), Radio Taiwan International, Focus Taiwan
- **Mainstream Media**: United Daily News, Liberty Times, TVBS, SET News, EBC (Eastern Broadcasting Company)
- **Digital Media**: The News Lens, CommonWealth Magazine, Reporter (報導者)

### 🏥 Healthcare

- **Government Agencies**: Taiwan Centers for Disease Control (CDC), National Health Insurance Administration (NHIA)
- **Medical Centers**: National Taiwan University Hospital, Veterans General Hospital, Chang Gung Medical Foundation

### 🤝 NGOs / Civil Society

- **Human Rights Organizations**: Taiwan Association for Human Rights
- **Environmental Groups**: Environmental Information Association, Taiwan Wilderness Society
- **Digital Rights**: Open Culture Foundation
- **Media Accountability**: Taiwan FactCheck Center

### 🎭 Arts and Culture Venues

- **Art Museums**: Taipei Fine Arts Museum, Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts
- **Performance Venues**: National Taichung Theater
- **Cultural and Creative Parks**: Songshan Cultural and Creative Park, Huashan 1914 Creative Park, Pier-2 Art Center

### 🤖 AI Corpora and Open Data

- **[Taiwan AI Training Corpus (TAIC)](https://taic.moda.gov.tw)**: Promoted by the Ministry of Digital Affairs, this corpus collects high-quality Traditional Chinese content from central government agencies, with 3,000+ datasets and over 1.1 billion tokens spanning six major categories: culture and arts, language and vocabulary, historical artifacts, local culture, tourism, and education. Available for application by enterprises and academic researchers. In the second half of 2026, the program will expand collaboration with the private sector.
- **Government Platforms**: Government Open Data Platform (data.gov.tw), Government Portal
- **Legal Databases**: National Laws and Regulations Database
- **Civic Participation**: g0v (gov-zero)
- **Cultural Archives**: Taiwan Cultural Memory Bank, National Repository of Cultural Heritage
